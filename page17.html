New Inspection Request

  • Requesting Agency Information
  • Property Information
  • Inspection Information
  • Notification Emails and Other Data

Contract ID

Agency-generated number to identify a specific inspection for a particular property address.

Requesting Department

Select the department responsible for the inspection request.

Contractor/Developer

This information is provided by the Requesting Agency.

Property Address

This information is provided by the Requesting Agency: Street Address, City, State, Zip Code, & Property Type

Property Contact

Contact information entered by the Requesting Agency includes- Home Owner and Home Owner Contact Number 

Point of Contact

The Requesting Agency enters the following information: Point of Contact, and Point of Contact Email.

Inspection Type

The Requesting Agency selects the Inspection Type: Pre-Bid, Initial Progress, Progress Meeting, Change Order, and Final.

Status & Date Requested

The system provides the status of the inspection report- Requested, Accepted, Scheduled, Completed. 

Inspector Info

The Requesting Agency selects the Assigned Inspector. Once the inspector confirms the receipt of the request via email, the Inspector's Name will automatically populate. 

Email

The Requesting Agency must enter the email address for the Requesting Analyst and the Inspection Coordinator, so that the system sends them notifications when needed.

Upload Documents

The Requesting Agency is allowed to upload relevant documents (.pdf, .doc, .docx, .txt, .xls)

Date Requested

The Requesting Agency is required to enter the date for requesting a property inspection.
