Inspector Reporting Interface

The inspector fills in the data:
Inspection Date Scheduled, Time of Inspection, Date [Report] Completed, Notes/Comments.
The following data is system-generated:
Inspection Agency, Inspector's Signature, Date of Inspection


The inspector is allowed to save their work and return later to complete their report, if needed.

Add Images to Scope of Work

Adding Images to a Scope of Work is simple.

Inspectors are allowed to use the camera on their mobile device or upload images from a local folder on their device. Images are saved in the system automatically. Inspectors choose which image to add to or remove from their reports.
