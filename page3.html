WAP Step 1
Confirmation Page

The applicant must confirm the following items, before they are allowed to apply for Weatherization Assistance.

  • Has the building been weatherized since 9/30/94 by the TN WAP Agnecy?- if the user select "Yes", the applicant is not eligible for this program. The system will allow the applicant to enter their information, but the application will be labeled ineligible. Click here to view.
  • The information entered by the applicant can be shared -The applicant must agree or the application will not be accepted.
  • Authorization of energy bill information release - The applicant must agree or the application will not be accepted.
